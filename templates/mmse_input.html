Mini-Mental State Examination (MMSE)

Orientation

The psychometrician should ask the patient about various aspects of their current environment and situation, such as the date, location, and other basic information, and assess whether the patient provides correct answers.

1. Date today

2. Year

3. Month

4. Day of the week

5. Season

6. Hospital

7. Floor

8. City

9. Area

10. State

Registration

The psychometrician should name three objects, allowing one second to say each one. Afterward, ask the patient to recall these objects and assess whether they recall them correctly.

11. Word 1 recall

12. Word 2 recall

13. Word 3 recall

Attention and Calculation

The psychometrician should ask the patient to spell the word "WORLD" backwards, and assess if the patient spells it correctly.

14. D

15. L

16. R

17. O

18. W

Recall

The psychometrician should ask the patient to recall the three words mentioned in the registration section and assess if they recall them correctly.

19. Word 1 delayed recall

20. Word 2 delayed recall

21. Word 3 delayed recall

Language

The psychometrician should point to their own pencil and watch, and ask the patient to name each as "pencil" and "watch" respectively.

22. Identify a watch

23. Identify a pencil

The psychometrician should ask the patient to repeat "No ifs, ands, or buts" and check whether done correctly.

24. Repeat "No ifs, ands, or buts"

The psychometrician should give the patient a three-stage command: "Take a paper in your hand, fold it in half, and put it on the floor." Assess whether the patient can follow these steps correctly.

25. Take a paper in your hand

26. Fold the paper in half

27. Put the paper on the floor

The psychometrician should write 'CLOSE YOUR EYES' on a piece of paper and tell the patient to read it aloud and obey the instruction.

28. Read and obey "Close your eyes"

The psychometrician should ask the patient to write any one complete sentence.

29. Write a sentence

The psychometrician should show the patient a design on the screen and ask them to copy it.

30. Copy the design shown below
